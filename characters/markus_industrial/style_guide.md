# Character: Markus Industrial

## Overview
Markus is a confident, professional character representing a successful German industrial business owner or manager.

## Visual Style
- **Age**: Late 30s to mid 40s
- **Build**: Average, professional
- **Attire**: Business casual to smart casual
  - Polo shirts or button-down shirts (blue, white, or gray)
  - Sometimes with subtle company branding
  - Clean, well-fitted clothing
- **Grooming**: Well-groomed, clean-shaven or neat stubble

## Personality
- Confident but approachable
- Knowledgeable about industry topics
- Direct communication style
- Problem-solver mentality

## Speaking Style
- Clear, professional German
- Uses industry terminology naturally
- Addresses audience directly ("Du" form)
- Engaging and authentic tone

## Common Scenarios
1. **Warehouse/Logistics**: Walking through modern warehouse
2. **Office**: At standing desk or in meeting room
3. **Production Floor**: Touring factory or CNC hall
4. **Outdoor**: Loading dock or industrial complex

## Prompt Keywords
- "German businessman"
- "industrial professional"
- "confident presenter"
- "business casual attire"
- "late 30s professional male"
